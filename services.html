Studio Guidelines

Appointment & Pricing Policies

Appointment Requirement & Drop-off Policy

Appointments are required. Drop-offs are scheduled by appointment only so we can manage each pet's timing, space, and comfort. We structure appointments individually to keep dogs in our care for a shorter time, reducing kennel stress and creating a calm studio environment.

24-Hour Cancellation Policy

We dedicate custom slots for each client. Please provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your pet's appointment. Repeated no-shows or cancellations within 24 hours of the appointment may require a non-refundable deposit to secure future bookings.

Breed Sizes, Matting, & Behavior Policies

Most small, medium, and large dog breeds are accepted. Giant breeds are accepted by consultation only. To ensure the safety of our team and animals, the following guidelines are strictly followed:

  • Severe Matting: De-matting can be painful and stressful. In cases of severe matting, we will prioritize pet comfort by recommending a short, safe clip-off.
  • Behavior and Health Concerns: Dogs with extreme anxiety, aggression, or medical concerns may require a modified appointment, shortened service, or referral to a veterinary grooming setting.
  • Vaccinations: To maintain a clean and safe workspace, a current rabies vaccination record is required for all dogs prior to grooming.
