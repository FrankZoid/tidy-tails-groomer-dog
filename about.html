Our Story

About Tidy Tails

Built on patient handling, clear communication, and a clean workspace for every dog's ultimate comfort.

Created For Pet Owners Who Care

Tidy Tails Pet Grooming was created for pet owners who want professional grooming in a calm, friendly environment. Our groomers love helping pets feel clean and comfortable, while giving owners clear expectations about timing, coat condition, pricing, and at-home maintenance.

Our Gentle Grooming Approach

We use patient handling, gentle communication, and a steady grooming process to help pets feel as comfortable as possible. Senior pets and anxious pets may need extra time, shorter appointments, or modified services based on their comfort level.

Cleanliness & Safety Standards

The grooming space is cleaned throughout the day, tools are sanitized between appointments, and pets are monitored carefully during services. We require current rabies vaccination records before grooming appointments. Pets with severe matting, medical issues, or extreme stress may require a modified service or veterinary grooming referral.
